HOW TO CREATE IN SAP:
---------------------
1. Open Transaction SE11
2. Select radio button: "Data type"
3. Enter name: ZSSODATA
4. Click "Create"
5. Select type: "Structure"
6. Enter Short Description: "Custom Structure for Sales Order ALV Report"
7. Add the following fields in the "Components" tab:
8. Save and Activate (Ctrl + F3)

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
  FIELD DEFINITIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

  No.  | Field Name | Data Element      | Short Description
  -----|------------|-------------------|----------------------------------
  1    | VBELN      | VBELN_VA          | Sales and Distribution Doc. No.
  2    | POSNR      | POSNR_VA          | Item Number of the SD Document
  3    | AUART      | AUART             | Sales Document Type
  4    | VKORG      | VKORG             | Sales Organization
  5    | VTWEG      | VTWEG             | Distribution Channel
  6    | SPART      | SPART             | Division
  7    | MATNR      | MATNR             | Material Number
  8    | PSTYV      | PSTYV             | Sales Document Item Category
  9    | ZMENG      | ZMENG             | Target Quantity in Sales Units
  10   | ZIEME      | VRKME             | Sales Unit of Measure
  11   | NETWR      | NETWR             | Net Value of the Order Item
  12   | WAERK      | WAERK_D           | Document Currency

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NOTES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

- All data elements listed above are standard SAP data elements available
  in any SAP ERP or S/4HANA system. No custom data elements are required.

- This structure is referenced in the ABAP report ZSOLIST via the
  parameter I_STRUCTURE_NAME = 'ZSSODATA' in the ALV method
  SET_TABLE_FOR_FIRST_DISPLAY. SAP automatically derives the field
  catalog (column labels, types, lengths) from this structure definition.

- The internal table GT_SOD in report ZSOLIST is declared as:
    DATA: gt_sod TYPE STANDARD TABLE OF zssodata

- Fields VBELN and POSNR together form the primary key of a sales order
  line item (header + item combination from VBAK and VBAP respectively).

- NETWR (Net Value) and WAERK (Currency) are paired fields — WAERK holds
  the currency unit for the monetary value stored in NETWR.

- ZMENG (Target Quantity) and ZIEME (Unit of Measure) are paired fields —
  ZIEME holds the unit for the quantity stored in ZMENG.
